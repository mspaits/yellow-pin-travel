About Yellow Pin Travel

I'm Jen, the founder of Yellow Pin Travel. I am an avid traveler with an insatiable appetite for planning travel. I have been across the globe in nearly every direction; my longest flight has been 14.5 hours in the air. I love every aspect of travel.

I have been planning our family travel since 2019. Using traditional cash bookings and points and miles I have taken the three of us to 6 countries and Hawai'i twice. We have upcoming travel to 4 countries and Alaska. I tailor all of our trips to be engaging so it warms my soul when my kid says he misses being somewhere we traveled.

Traditional Cash Bookings

I am an advisor with Fora and can work with you to tailor your perfect holiday. Fora has first class connections to get you upgrades and perks at hotels; I can also reserve those memorable activities and comforts that round out travel.

Points and Miles

I am well versed in the points and miles world and am happy to be your beacon. I can do your award flight search, find the perfect hotel on points, or teach you how to all works if you are new to this world.

Why Pins?

I decided when my son was born that we would get an enamel pin from every place he visited. They don't take up space in luggage, are colorful, and importantly, are easy to store in a jar (or two or three) at home.
